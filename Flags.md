# Flags

This is a comprehensive framework for financial analysis. To make this actionable, I have assigned **Quantitative Thresholds (Benchmarks)** to each flag based on industry standards used by credit analysts, forensic accountants, and institutional investors.

# üî¥ RED FLAGS: QUANTITATIVE STANDARDS

### 1Ô∏è‚É£ Cash Flow & Liquidity Red Flags

- **Kangde Xin Paradox**: Cash > 20% of Total Assets while Interest Expense > 5% of Total Debt.
  
- **Cash vs. Profit Divergence**: Net Income grows > 10% while OCF is flat or declining.
  
- **OCF/NI Ratio**: Operating Cash Flow / Net Income **< 0.8** for 3 consecutive years.
  
- **Volatility Gap**: StDev of OCF / StDev of Revenue **> 2.5x**.
  
- **Negative FCF**: FCF is negative for **> 3 years** despite positive Net Income.
  
- **Restricted Cash**: Restricted Cash **> 15%** of Total Cash.
  
- **Offshore Trapped Cash**: Cash held in foreign subsidiaries **> 50%** of total cash with high local tax hurdles.
  
- **Window Dressing**: Cash balance at year-end is **> 30% higher** than the average of the previous 3 quarters.
  
- **Factoring Reliance**: Factored receivables **> 20%** of total Accounts Receivable.
  
- **Payables Stretch**: Days Payable Outstanding (DPO) increases by **> 20% YoY** without a change in supplier terms.
  

### 2Ô∏è‚É£ Revenue Quality & Recognition Red Flags

- **VAT‚ÄìRevenue Gap**: Revenue growth exceeds VAT/Sales Tax paid by **> 15%**.
  
- **AR vs. Revenue Growth**: (Œî AR / Œî Revenue) **> 2.0**.
  
- **Quarter-End Spikes**: Q4 Revenue **> 35%** of Annual Revenue (for non-seasonal businesses).
  
- **Collection Lag**: Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) exceeds the industry average by **> 30%**.
  
- **Non-Core Revenue**: "Other Income" **> 15%** of EBIT.
  
- **Policy Change**: Any change that accelerates revenue (e.g., moving from "point in time" to "over time").
  
- **Related-Party Revenue**: Sales to related parties **> 10%** of total revenue.
  
- **Negative Deferred Revenue**: Deferred Revenue declines by **> 10%** while Revenue increases.
  
- **Post-Period Returns**: Sales returns in Q1 **> 10%** of Q4 revenue.
  
- **Customer Concentration**: Single customer **> 30%** or top 5 customers **> 60%** of revenue.
  

### 3Ô∏è‚É£ Cost Structure & Margin Red Flags

- **Unexplained Margin Expansion**: Gross Margin expansion **> 500 bps** without corresponding volume or price increases.
  
- **Capitalization Ratio**: (Capitalized R&D / Total R&D) **> 50%**.
  
- **Industry Divergence**: Company margins rise while industry margins fall by **> 200 bps**.
  
- **OPEX Divergence**: Revenue growth **> 10%** while SG&A is flat or declining.
  
- **Recurring "One-Offs"**: Non-recurring charges appearing in **3 out of 5** years.
  
- **Restructuring Frequency**: Restructuring charges in **> 40%** of reporting periods over 5 years.
  
- **Expense Reclassification**: Significant shifts from OPEX to COGS (lowering Gross Margin but hiding OPEX growth).
  
- **Margin Divergence**: Gross Margin increases while Operating Margin decreases.
  

### 4Ô∏è‚É£ Receivables, Inventory & Working Capital Red Flags

- **Rising DSO**: DSO increases by **> 15 days** YoY.
  
- **Inventory vs. Sales**: (Œî Inventory % / Œî Revenue %) **> 1.5**.
  
- **Inventory Obsolescence**: Inventory turnover ratio (COGS / Avg Inventory) declines by **> 20% YoY**.
  
- **Prepaid Expense Spike**: Prepaids / Total Assets **> 5%**.
  
- **Payables Reliance**: DPO growth is the sole driver of positive OCF.
  
- **Provision Reversal**: Reversal of previous write-downs accounts for **> 10%** of Net Income.
  
- **Other Current Assets**: "Other Current Assets" **> 10%** of Total Current Assets.
  
- **AR Aging**: Receivables "Past Due > 90 days" **> 20%** of total AR.
  

### 5Ô∏è‚É£ Asset Quality & Capitalization Red Flags

- **Capex/Revenue Mismatch**: Capex / Revenue **< 0.5x** of industry peers while claiming high growth.
  
- **Intangibles/Earnings**: (Œî Intangibles / Œî NI) **> 2.0**.
  
- **Goodwill Density**: Goodwill / Total Assets **> 40%** or Goodwill / Equity **> 100%**.
  
- **Repeated Impairment**: Impairments taken within **24 months** of an acquisition.
  
- **Revaluation Gains**: Revaluation reserves **> 20%** of Total Equity.
  
- **Useful Life**: Depreciation as % of Gross PPE is **< 75%** of the peer average.
  
- **Capitalized Interest**: Capitalized Interest **> 10%** of total Interest Expense.
  
- **Idle Assets**: Asset Turnover (Revenue / Total Assets) declines for **3 consecutive years**.
  

### 6Ô∏è‚É£ Debt, Leverage & Off-Balance-Sheet Red Flags

- **Leverage vs. Profit**: Total Debt / EBITDA **> 4.0x** while reporting record profits.
  
- **Debt Cliff**: Short-term Debt / Total Debt **> 50%**.
  
- **Covenant Proximity**: EBITDA / Interest Expense is within **10%** of the loan covenant limit.
  
- **Related-Party Loans**: Loans to/from related parties **> 5%** of Total Assets.
  
- **SPV Exposure**: Presence of "Variable Interest Entities" (VIEs) with significant debt.
  
- **Lease Mismatch**: Lease liabilities growing while headcount/locations are shrinking.
  
- **FX Mismatch**: Foreign Currency Debt **> 30%** of total debt with no corresponding foreign revenue.
  
- **Funding Mismatch**: Using Short-term Debt to fund Capex (ST Debt / Capex **> 1.0**).
  

### 7Ô∏è‚É£ Earnings Manipulation Signals (Classic Models)

- **Beneish M-Score**: Score **> -1.78** (Probability of manipulation).
  
- **Total Accruals**: (Net Income - OCF) / Total Assets **> 10%**.
  
- **Analyst Beating**: Earnings surprise is consistently between **+1% and +3%**.
  
- **Volatility Ratio**: StDev of Operating Income / StDev of OCF **< 0.7**.
  
- **Big Bath**: One-year loss **> 3x** average annual profit, followed by a **> 20%** ROE.
  
- **Depreciation Slowdown**: Depreciation expense as % of PPE drops by **> 15%** without asset sales.
  

### 8Ô∏è‚É£ Governance & Disclosure Red Flags

- **Auditor Turnover**: Auditor change within the last **24 months**.
  
- **Audit Opinion**: "Qualified" or "Emphasis of Matter" regarding Going Concern.
  
- **Compensation Logic**: CEO bonus is **> 80%** dependent on a single metric (e.g., EPS).
  
- **Related Party Volume**: Total RPT volume **> 5%** of total expenses.
  
- **Segment Obscurity**: Reducing the number of reportable segments.
  
- **Filing Delays**: Filing within **5 days** of the legal deadline or requesting extensions.
  
- **Founder Power**: Founder is CEO + Chairman and owns **> 50%** of voting shares.
  
- **Word Count**: MD&A length increases by **> 20%** with "boilerplate" or defensive language.

# üü¢ GREEN FLAGS: QUANTITATIVE STANDARDS

### 1Ô∏è‚É£ Cash Flow & Earnings Quality Green Flags

- **Cash-Backed Profits**: OCF / Net Income consistently **> 1.1x**.
  
- **FCF Resilience**: Positive FCF even during revenue declines of **up to 10%**.
  
- **Cash Conversion Cycle (CCC)**: CCC is stable or decreasing by **> 2 days per year**.
  
- **Dividend Coverage**: (OCF - Capex) / Dividends **> 2.0x**.
  
- **Organic Working Capital**: Working Capital growth is **< Revenue growth**.
  
- **Interest Coverage**: EBITDA / Interest Expense **> 8.0x**.
  

### 2Ô∏è‚É£ Revenue Quality Green Flags

- **Organic Sync**: Growth rates of Revenue, AR, and Inventory are within **5%** of each other.
  
- **Deferred Revenue**: Growth in Deferred Revenue **‚â•** Growth in Revenue.
  
- **Retention**: Net Revenue Retention (NRR) **> 100%**.
  
- **Capacity Alignment**: Revenue growth is within **1.2x** of Capex/Capacity growth.
  
- **Consistent Policies**: No change in revenue recognition for **> 5 years**.
  
- **Diversification**: No single customer **> 10%** of revenue.
  

### 3Ô∏è‚É£ Cost Discipline & Margin Quality Green Flags

- **Scale Efficiency**: Operating Margin improves by **< 100 bps** for every 10% revenue growth (realistic scaling).
  
- **Stable Ratios**: SG&A as % of Revenue stays within a **+/- 2%** band.
  
- **Full Expensing**: 100% of R&D and Maintenance Capex is expensed immediately.
  
- **Minimal Adjustments**: "Adjusted EBITDA" is within **10%** of Statutory EBITDA.
  

### 4Ô∏è‚É£ Working Capital Health Green Flags

- **Stable DSO**: DSO variance **< 5 days** over 3 years.
  
- **Inventory Turnover**: DIO is within **10%** of the 5-year median.
  
- **Provisioning**: Allowance for Doubtful Accounts / Total AR is stable or increasing slightly.
  
- **Transparent Aging**: Full disclosure of AR aging (0‚Äì30, 31‚Äì60, etc.).
  

### 5Ô∏è‚É£ Asset Quality Green Flags

- **ROIC vs. WACC**: ROIC consistently **> WACC + 3%**.
  
- **Goodwill Buffer**: Goodwill / Equity **< 20%**.
  
- **Early Impairment**: Management takes impairments during industry-wide downturns, not after.
  
- **High Turnover**: Asset Turnover is in the **top quartile** of the industry.
  

### 6Ô∏è‚É£ Capital Structure & Funding Green Flags

- **Self-Funding**: (OCF / Capex) **> 1.5x**.
  
- **Conservative Leverage**: Net Debt / EBITDA **< 2.0x**.
  
- **Debt Ladder**: No more than **20%** of debt matures in any single year.
  
- **Covenant Cushion**: Minimum **30% headroom** on all financial covenants.
  

### 7Ô∏è‚É£ Governance & Reporting Quality Green Flags

- **Big 4 Tenure**: Reputable auditor for **> 10 years**.
  
- **Clarity**: MD&A provides specific volume vs. price breakdowns for revenue.
  
- **Guidance Accuracy**: Actual results within **+/- 5%** of management guidance.
  
- **Board Independence**: **> 75%** independent board members.
  

### 8Ô∏è‚É£ Valuation & Market Signal Green Flags

- **"Real" Buybacks**: Shares outstanding decrease by **> 2% YoY** funded entirely by FCF.
  
- **Insider Conviction**: Net insider buying (not just options) over the last **6 months**.
  
- **Steady ROIC**: ROIC variance **< 3%** over a 5-year cycle.
